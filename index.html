Who we are

Greater Bridgeport Community Enterprises, Inc. (GBCE), founded December 14th 2006, is a non-profit 501c3 community development corporation dedicated to creating green jobs for our community's disadvantaged residents, and particularly those who were formerly incarcerated, by creating social impact ventures that contribute to environmental sustainability as well as the financial security of our families and community.

Our mission

At our Park City Green (PCG) division, our mission is to partner with companies, municipalities, organizations and institutions to save them money in mattress disposal, increase their recycling rates, green and clean neighborhoods, and empower communities and individuals economically through job creation.
